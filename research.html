Research

My research interests lie at the interface of biomaterials, drug delivery systems, and immuno-engineering.

Microneedle-based Drug Delivery

I am interested in microneedle-based platforms for localized and sustained drug delivery, particularly for superficial tumors such as melanoma.

Biomaterials for Immuno-engineering

I am interested in designing biomaterials that can modulate immune responses and improve local therapeutic delivery.

Drug Delivery Systems

I am broadly interested in hydrogels, polymeric materials, lipid nanoparticles, and minimally invasive delivery platforms.
